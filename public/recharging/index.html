recharging

we were building npmx at a pace that was costing some all of us sleep. we didn't want that to be the norm! so we took a week off. together.

what happened

discord was closed February 14 – 21.

all invite links were gone and channels were locked – except #garden, which stayed open for folks who wanted to keep hanging out.

in the meantime

npmx.dev and the repo stayed open – people still dug in, filed some issues, opened a few PRs, but mainly everyone spent time somewhere near a cosy fireplace.

we came back!

we came back recharged and ready for the final push to March 3rd. follow us on Bluesky for updates.
